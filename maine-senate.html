Maine draws Senate districts. There are 35 of them and they all have around 40,000 people.
This number isn't fixed. The Constitution says there must be 'an odd number of Senators, not less than 31 nor more than 35'. Which seems like a really messy way to say 31, 33, or 35. Also, they mean "fewer", not "less".
The Senate districts are numbered from north to south, whereas the House districts are numbered the other direction.
