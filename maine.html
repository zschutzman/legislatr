Maine

Maine has 2 Congressional districts, 35 State Senate districts, and 151 State House districts.

Click a district to see its info


About this tool

Use this interactive map to explore the current districting plans in Maine for the U.S House of Representatives as well as both houses of the state's legislature, the State House of Representatives and the State Senate. Clicking on a district will provide additional details about it. Use the geographic maps to see an accurate picture of what the districts look like. The hexagonal cartograms represent each district with a single hexagon, all of the same size. This gives a good visualization of the partisan breakdown of the various legislative delegations.

This page was created by Zachary Schutzman based on work at the Voting Rights Data Institute, and is being edited and maintained by MGGG.

The project extends work done by Ethan Ackerman, Zach Levitt, Andrew Medeiros, Mack Meller, Onuoha Odim, Sherlock Ortiz, Sarita Rosenstock, and Kaki Ryan.

These maps were made using Leaflet and use qgis2web as a template.
